Our signature chicken is marinated in a blend of secret spices, then grilled to perfection over an open flame, locking in those smoky, juicy flavors. Whether you love classic BBQ, spicy peri-peri, honey garlic, lemon herb, spicy, tangy, or classic, we’ve got a flavor that fits your cravings and perfect match for your taste buds. Whole, half, wings, or as a grilled chicken sandwich, our menu gives you options. Pair it with our freshly made sides for the ultimate meal!
